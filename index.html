Roll the dice:

First to score 100 points win the game.

How to play:

On each turn, you throw a die and scores as many points as the total shown on the die.

If you don't roll a 1, you have to decide to keep your current score or throw the die again to accumulate more points.

If you roll a 1, you lose all your current score and your turn is over. The game continue until a player reach 100 points.
